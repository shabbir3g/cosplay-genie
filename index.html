about cosplay genie

Cosplay Genie is a NODWIN Gaming property dedicated to the art of Cosplay. With an objective to recognize, build and reward cosplayers of the country, NODWIN Gaming endeavours to mould all-inclusive gaming and esports ecosystem.

Since its inception in 2015, Cosplay Genie has been actively involved with the Indian cosplay community through NODWIN Gaming operated cosplay competitions at various flagships like the ESL India Premiership and DreamHack India. Our exclusive partnership with World Cosplay Summit for the India Qualifiers marks a major milestone for the organisation and the community alike. NODWIN actively incorporates cosplay in its gaming and esports events as an effort to build the cosplay community on every big platform possible.

Cosplay Genie is a platform for community engagement and learning. Rich content around the beautiful art form is a way to drive awareness and bring cosplay to the mainstream. Live sessions with top cosplayers, online contests, DIYs and costume tutorials are a major part of the outreach.
